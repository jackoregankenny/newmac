newmac

Your Mac, your way — pick a stack, fine-tune, install.

$ bash -c "$(curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jackoregankenny/newmac/main/install)"

Fresh Mac, nothing pre-installed. Installs the Command Line Tools, then opens an interactive picker. Re-runnable and idempotent.
